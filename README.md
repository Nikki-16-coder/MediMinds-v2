# 🧠 MEDIMINDS

> “Care for your mind like you care for your body.”

MEDIMINDS is an AI and Blockchain-powered mental wellness platform designed to provide secure, personalized, and intelligent mental health support. The project focuses on emotional well-being, stress management, mood tracking, and women’s wellness through technology-driven solutions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Mental health is one of the most important aspects of human well-being, yet many people hesitate to seek help due to fear, lack of awareness, or limited access to support systems.

MEDIMINDS combines:

- 🤖 Artificial Intelligence (AI)
- 🔐 Blockchain Technology
- 💬 Smart Chat Support
- 📊 Mood & Symptom Tracking
- 🌸 Women Wellness Features

to create a safe and supportive digital mental wellness ecosystem.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Many individuals suffering from:

- Anxiety
- Depression
- Stress
- Emotional burnout

often remain undiagnosed because symptoms are subtle or ignored.

Additional challenges include:

- Lack of time to seek professional help
- Fear of judgment or insecurity
- Poor emotional awareness
- Limited integration of women’s hormonal and mental health tracking

MEDIMINDS aims to solve these problems through smart, secure, and accessible technology.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🤖 AI-Based Mental Wellness Support
- Detects early signs of stress, anxiety, and depression
- AI chatbot available 24/7 for support
- Sentiment and emotional analysis
- Personalized self-help recommendations
- Predictive analytics for high-risk detection

### 🌸 Women Wellness Tracking
- Menstrual cycle prediction
- Mood tracking during cycle phases
- Symptom logging:
  - Cramps
  - Mood swings
  - Flow intensity
- Reminders and alerts for periods and ovulation

### 🔐 Blockchain Integration
- Secure and private data storage
- Anonymous and trusted sharing
- Immutable wellness records
- Smart contracts for therapy-related services

### 🎵 Sound Therapy Support
AI can recommend therapeutic frequency sounds based on emotional analysis and securely track user progress.
